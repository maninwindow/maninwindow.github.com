2019 Spring Outing

April 22, 2019

    Last Saturday, CCTMCers went outing at the Olympic Forest Park. We had a great time burning calories and taking snapshots despite the cold and mizzly weather

Warm up session

    Participants were devided into three teams: Red, Blue and Yellow. Let’s do some warming-up together!

Walking race session

Rules of the Game
    1. Both roaming and jogging are ok; each team’s result is based on the last-arriving member’s record;

    2. Each team should finish two shooting & photography tasks

  - Take a picture with elements of tower, plants, water and all the team members

  - Shoot a 20-sec video with an animal/kid and all the team members

Relax & Frisbee

    After the race, participants stretched their body and practiced frisbee at the lawn.
